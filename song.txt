Nippu ra thakara sadyama

Nippu ra thakara chooddam

Thakithe mase kada motham

Durathmula duragatham nithyam

Perigithe ragadam thadyam

Jaganike talonchani toofani

Jananikai janminchina nestanni

Vidhine gelavada eesooli

Ushassule parichedu Kabali Kabali


Karunalu bali Kalathalika veli

Manusudikinda ukkuladilu

Antha nedu maye maye

Nee souryam nithyam samaramaye

Nee rajyamlona ragile rosham

Prathi mataku kotha paramardam
